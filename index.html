Privacy Policy

At ThuTalk, your privacy is our core priority. We have engineered this platform to ensure that your social and dating experiences are governed by high-level security and transparency.

1. Information We Collect

To provide a seamless experience, we collect specific data points that allow the system to function effectively:

  • Account Data: Username, profile picture, and gender preferences.
  • Communications: Real-time chat messages and media shared within the app.
  • Usage Metadata: Interaction logs and reporting history to maintain community safety.

2. How We Protect Your Data

We implement a multi-layered security approach to keep your information private:

  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification to advertisers or third parties.
  • Secure Hosting: All data is hosted on secure PythonAnywhere servers with restricted access.
  • Real-time Moderation: Our integrated Telegram bot alerts the development team to reports instantly to ensure a safe environment.

3. User Controls & Rights

You are in total control of your ThuTalk experience. You have the right to:

  • Block/Unblock: Instantly restrict any user from contacting you.
  • Report Abuse: Flag inappropriate behavior directly to our administrators.
  • Data Deletion: Request the removal of your account and associated chat history.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

We use local storage primarily for technical purposes, such as maintaining your login session and remembering your UI preferences (like Dark Mode).

5. Policy Updates

As ThuTalk evolves, we may update this policy. We recommend checking this page periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
